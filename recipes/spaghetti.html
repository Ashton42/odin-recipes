Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta sauce

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef until brown, set aside.
  2. Bring water to boil and then put in spaghetti noodles. Cook 8-10 minutes or until tender.
  3. Strain pasta and return to pan.
  4. Mix pasta sauce, ground beef, and spaghetti noodles. Serve and enjoy.
