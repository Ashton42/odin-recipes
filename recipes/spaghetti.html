Spaghetti

Spaghetti noodles with pasta sauce and ground beef.

Spaghetti is a classic for good reason. It is cheap and delicious, and can be made in a veriety of ways. You can't go wrong with this easy to make recipe.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta sauce
  1. Cook ground beef until brown, set aside.
  2. Bring water to boil and then put in spaghetti noodles. Cook 8-10 minutes or until tender.
  3. Strain pasta and return to pan.
  4. Mix pasta sauce, ground beef, and spaghetti noodles. Serve and enjoy.
