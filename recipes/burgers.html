Burgers

Classic hamburger with lettuce, tomato, pickle, onion.

Another simple meal to make at home when you dont feel like cooking a full meal. Make it your way by adding veggies, cheese, bacon, or even a fried egg.

Ingredients

  • 6-8oz Ground beef
  • Letteuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickle
  • Onion
  • Hamburger bun

Directions

  1. Shape ground beef into ball and then press in to patty
  2. Season patty to liking and cook on medium heat until brown. Flip and cook other side until brown. Repeat until fully cooked.
  3. Set cooked patty on burger bun and top with desired condiments and veggies and enjoy.
