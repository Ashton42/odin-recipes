Burgers

Classic hamburger patty with the usual veggies.

Ingredients

  • 6-8oz Ground beef
  • Letteuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickle
  • Onion
  • Hamburger bun

Directions

  1. Shape ground beef into ball and then press in to patty
  2. Season patty to liking and cook on medium heat until brown. Flip and cook other side until brown. Repeat until fully cooked.
  3. Set cooked patty on burger bun and top with desired condiments and veggies and enjoy.
