Tacos

Seasoned ground beef with cheese and veggies of your choice rolled up in to a flour tortilla.

A favorite by many, and for good reason. Some of the best ingredients all piled together inside of a crunchy taco shell.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Taco shellls

Directions

  1. Brown 1 pound of ground beef.
  2. Drain grease, if necessary.
  3. Add packet of taco seasoning.
  4. Stir and portion onto taco shells.
  5. Top with veggies and cheese and serve.
